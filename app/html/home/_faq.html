Frequently asked. Memetically answered.

  • What is MyBNBSocial?

    MyBNBSocial is a Web3-native social platform built on the BNB Chain.
    It’s a place where traders, meme creators, and crypto communities connect through posts, memes, and alpha — all on-chain and without the noise of traditional social media.

  • Is it like X (Twitter)?

    Not quite. There are no ads, no shadowbans, and no algorithm deciding what you see.
    It’s a decentralized, user-first experience focused on relevance, not reach.

  • Who’s it for?

    For anyone living the Web3 lifestyle:
    Degens, NFT collectors, alpha hunters, memers, builders, and communities looking for a space that understands crypto culture natively.

  • How do I join the beta?

    Hit the “Get Early Access” button, fill out the form, and we’ll notify selected users with private access.
    Early testers will get to help shape features — and secure early perks.

  • Is it free to use?

    100% free during beta.
    Later on, premium features like token rewards or NFT-based identities may be added, but the core feed will remain free for everyone.

  • Can I post memes or just alpha?

    Both are welcome — and both are needed.
    Some users post market breakdowns, some drop daily memes. The culture is shaped by you.

  • Will I keep my @handle after beta?

    Yes. Handles are reserved during beta.
    If you claim @yourname early, it’s yours when the full version goes live. First come, first minted.

  • Will there be token/NFT rewards?

    We’re planning integrations with airdrops, community contests, and limited-edition NFTs for top contributors and early buzzers. Beta users will get access to exclusive perks first.

  • When is the full launch?

    We’re testing with a tight group of early users right now.
    Public access will roll out in phases — beta users will be first in line when it happens.

Buzz us at support@mybnb.social if something’s still unclear.
